As the eerie echo of her name reverberated through the abandoned asylum, Dr. Roberts rushed towards the door where his patient had been last seen, only to find it slowly creaking open by itself, revealing a vacant wheelchair that had once belonged to Morgan, and a note scrawled on the wall in crimson letters: Another day. Another day to start all over again. The words seemed to sear themselves into Dr. Roberts' mind as he heard the faint whisper of Morgan's voice repeating its haunting refrain, drawing him deeper into the darkness within.
